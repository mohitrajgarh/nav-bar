Academics at Alwar Public School

Alwar Public School offers a comprehensive academic program to foster intellectual growth and creativity. Our curriculum is designed to inspire students and prepare them for a successful future in their chosen fields.

Primary Education

Our primary school focuses on building foundational skills in subjects like math, science, and languages.

Secondary Education

A diverse curriculum for secondary students that includes advanced studies in various subjects to prepare for higher education.

Extracurricular Activities

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, arts, and drama to promote well-rounded development.
