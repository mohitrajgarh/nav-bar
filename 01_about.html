About Alwar Public School

Alwar Public School, established in 1995, is a premier institution that aims to provide quality education to students, fostering an environment of growth, creativity, and learning. We pride ourselves on our dedicated faculty, excellent facilities, and a commitment to nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in an ever-evolving world. We believe in creating a holistic learning experience that nurtures intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and personal development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a leading institution that prepares students to be responsible global citizens and champions of innovation, compassion, and excellence. We aim to provide a well-rounded education that caters to the academic, emotional, and social growth of every child.
